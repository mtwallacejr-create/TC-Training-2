2. LOAN FROM LENDER. Lender agrees to provide Buyer with the Deposit to open escrow with the Escrow Agent as required by Buyer's purchase contract with Seller.

3. REPAYMENT. Buyer shall repay the Deposit and cause Lender to be paid the Lender Compensation upon the close of escrow, whether through the listed escrow or a Double Escrow.

4. IRREVOCABLE ESCROW INSTRUCTIONS. Buyer irrevocably instructs Escrow Agent to pay the Deposit plus Lender Compensation from Buyer's proceeds at closing.

5. DEFAULT. If the contract is cancelled and Deposit + Lender Fees are not repaid within five (5) days, Buyer owes the Deposit + Lender Fees plus 25% per annum interest from the Effective Date.

6. SECURITY. Repayment is secured by a lien against any escrow funds held for Buyer's benefit, evidenced by a UCC-1 financing statement filed in the state where the Property is located.
